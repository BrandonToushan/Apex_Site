Join Our Team

Apex is currently recruiting the inaugural cohort of its flagship fellowship program. The ideal candidates will be self-motivated, driven, deeply knowledgeable about technology and intensely focused on pursuing a future career in capital markets, corporate strategy or data science

Our fellows can expect to commit upwards of 20+ hours per week to both the fellowship and Apex's unique development program. Apex Fellows will have the opportunity to refine their professional skillsets and accelerate their careers by working hand in hand with Apex principals on a wide variety of extremely visible, high impact engagements across Apex's three main verticals.

Interested candidates should send an up-to-date resume and cover letter to alex@apexadvisory.ca

Please note that Apex Fellowships are only open to students currently enrolled at Canadian Universities at this time.
